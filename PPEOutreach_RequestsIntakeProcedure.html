Procedure:

  1. Facilities include:
  1. Nursing homes
  2. Group homes
  3. Assisted living facilities
  4. Hospitals
  5. Clinics

  1. Search for facilities listed in the following websites:
  2. https://www.health.state.mn.us/facilities/regulation/directory/providerselect.html
  3. https://getusppe.org/ which leads to https://findthemasks.com/
  4. Donategoodstuff.org
  5. https://www.seniorlivingfacilities.net/assisted-living/minnesota/big-lake.html
  6. http://donateppe.org/

  1. Once you identify the facility, find their contact information and call them.

  1. On the call:
  1. Introduce yourself and mention the organization you are representing, IAM-Rotary.
  2. Share that you are donating PPE and want to learn about their PPE product needs.
  3. Here are the products that we carry:
  1. Fabric Masks
  2. Face Shields
  3. Hand Sanitizer
  4. Disinfectant Wipes
  5. Fabric Gowns
  6. Nitrile Gloves
  7. Disposable Masks
  8. KN95 Masks
  9. Oximeters
  10. Thermometers
  11. Goggles
  1. Inquire about the number of beds as a indicator of the size of the facility and capture in the corresponding Distribution Center spreadsheet
  2. Please record their requirement details in the corresponding spreadsheet - EP Distribution Center or MG Distribution Center
  1. Products and count
  2. Contact person and phone number
  3. Delivery instructions
  1. The order delivery group will take it from there.
